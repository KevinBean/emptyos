Using AI to Write Posts
Recommended workflow:
  1. Outline first — Type your topic, select all, click "Outline" to get a structure
  2. Draft freely — Write rough content under each heading without worrying about polish
  3. Expand — Select thin sections and click "Expand" to add depth
  4. Polish — Select all and click "Polish" for grammar and flow
  5. Compress — If too long, select verbose sections and click "Compress"
  6. Suggest Title — Click to auto-generate title and SEO summary
  7. Translate — Select text to translate between Chinese and English
Suggest from Vault: Click "Suggest from Vault" in the template bar. AI scans your notes and recommends the best candidates for blog posts. Click a suggestion to load it as a starting draft.
