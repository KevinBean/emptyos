Gesture cheat sheet

✋Open_PalmHold 1s → open palette + listen
✊Closed_FistCancel listening · hold 2s to exit
👍Thumb_UpFire now / confirm
👎Thumb_DownRetry / undo (within 3s)
☝️Pointing_UpHold → finger-tip scroll (move hand up/down)
✌️VictoryQuick-capture (skip palette)
🤟ILoveYouApp-registered favorite / approve
Pages can override the three action gestures by calling EOS.handsFree.registerGesture(name, fn, desc). Example: the task app binds Pointing_Up to "complete top focus task" when you're on /task/.
