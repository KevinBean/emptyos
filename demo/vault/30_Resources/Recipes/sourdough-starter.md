## Day 1
50g flour + 50g water in a clean jar. Stir, cover loosely, leave at room temp.

## Days 2-7
Once a day: discard half, feed with 50g flour + 50g water.
By day 5-7 it should be doubling within 6-8 hours of feeding.

## Notes

Tap water with chlorine kills the wild yeast. Use filtered or sit-overnight tap water. If it smells acetone-y after 3 days that's normal — it'll calm down by day 5.
