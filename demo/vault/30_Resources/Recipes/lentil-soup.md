## Ingredients

- 1 cup brown or green lentils
- 1 large onion, diced
- 3 cloves garlic, minced
- 2 carrots, diced
- 2 celery stalks, diced
- 1 tsp cumin
- 1 tsp smoked paprika
- 1 can (400g) crushed tomatoes
- 1 L vegetable stock
- Juice of 1 lemon
- Olive oil, salt, pepper
- Optional: handful of spinach at the end

## Method

1. Sweat the onion + carrot + celery in olive oil, ~8 minutes, until soft.
2. Add garlic + cumin + paprika, stir 1 minute.
3. Add lentils, tomatoes, stock. Simmer 25-30 minutes until lentils are tender.
4. Off heat: lemon juice + salt + spinach. Taste and adjust.

## Notes

The lemon at the end is the difference between a fine soup and one you'll keep making.
