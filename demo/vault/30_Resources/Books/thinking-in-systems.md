## Notes

Read this twice. The second pass landed differently — once you've seen a few feedback loops in the wild, the chapter on "leverage points" reads like a manual.

## Highlights

> Hunger, poverty, environmental degradation, economic instability... and many others, all derive from one source: an inability to think systemically.

> The most important leverage point of any system is the paradigm out of which the system arises.
