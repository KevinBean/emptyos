## What to try in 5 minutes

1. **Search** — press `/` and type any word — you'll see results across Apps + Notes + a "Open full search" link.
2. **Tasks** (`/task/`) — add a task, watch it appear in the Hub's "Today's Tasks" panel.
3. **Journal** (`/journal/`) — write today's entry, hit Submit. Check the heatmap.
4. **Aura** (`/voice-assistant/`) — type "list my tasks" or click REC and speak. The local model is small (qwen2.5:1.5b) — slow but coherent. For better quality, paste an OpenAI key in Settings → Demo → BYOK.
5. **Music Library** (`/music-library/`) — browse the seed songs; try writing AI lyrics for a new song.

## Daily reset

Anything you create here is wiped at 04:00 UTC daily. The demo always starts from the same seed content.
